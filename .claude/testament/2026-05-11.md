## typescript-config is the 12th package directory, not published

`packages/` contains 12 directories. The 12th is `typescript-config` — shared TypeScript config (base.json, strict.json), no src, no tests, not published to npm. Mission scope is always 11 packages.

## Test conventions are not uniform across packages

Two patterns in use:

- **`src/**/*.test.ts`** (co-located): `build-azure-local-settings` only.
- **`test/**/*.spec.ts`** (separate test dir): everything else with tests.

Each `vitest.config.ts` declares its include pattern explicitly — don't assume one pattern applies everywhere.

## build-clean and graphql-codegen-treeshake have no test files

Both have `vitest.config.ts` but no `test/` directory. `build-clean`'s `package.json` doesn't define a `test` script at all.

## core-di vs core-di-lite: eager vs configurable

The distinction is not just size. `core-di-lite` builds all services eagerly at `buildProvider()` time, singletons only, no lifetimes or scopes, throws `DuplicateRegistrationError` on duplicate registration. `core-di` has lifetimes, scoped providers, `resolveAll`, `IServiceModule`, `Symbol.dispose`, and override support.
