## Decisions

- **changes.jsonl starts at 5.0.0**: the package was forked from willmorgan/bragma. Pre-5.0.0 entries are upstream history. A "forked from" entry records the lineage.
- **Formatter disabled in per-package biome.json**: the source was never biome-formatted under the new config. Organize imports also disabled. Linter rules preserved from old repo.
- **Example tsconfig.check.json depth**: examples are 3 levels deep (`examples/<repo>/<sub>/`), not 2 like packages.
